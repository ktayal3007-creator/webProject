# FINDIT.AI - Quick Start Guide

## For Users Who Lost Something

1. **Browse Lost Items**
   - Click "Lost Items" in the navigation
   - Use the search bar to find your item
   - Filter by date range if needed
   - Click on any item to see full details and contact information

2. **Report a Lost Item**
   - Click "Report Lost" in the navigation
   - Fill out the form with item details:
     - Item name (required)
     - Description (required)
     - Category (required)
     - Date lost (required)
     - Location (required)
     - Campus (required)
     - Your contact information (required)
   - Submit the form
   - Your report will appear in "My Reports" sidebar
   - Your report is now searchable by others

## For Users Who Found Something

1. **Browse Found Items**
   - Click "Found Items" in the navigation
   - Use the search bar to check if someone is looking for the item
   - Filter by date range if needed
   - Click on any item to see full details

2. **Report a Found Item**
   - Click "Report Found" in the navigation
   - Fill out the form with item details:
     - Item name (required)
     - Description (required)
     - Category (required)
     - Date found (required)
     - Location (required)
     - Campus (required)
     - Your contact information (required)
   - Submit the form
   - Your report will appear in "My Reports" sidebar
   - Item owners can now find and contact you

## Searching for Items

### Lost Items Search
- Searches through: item names, descriptions, categories, locations, and campuses
- Updates in real-time as you type
- Use date range filter to narrow results
- Results show most recent items first

### Found Items Search
- Independent from lost items search
- Searches through: item names, descriptions, categories, locations, and campuses
- Updates in real-time as you type
- Use date range filter to narrow results
- Results show most recent items first

## Viewing Item Details

Click on any item card to see:
- Full description
- Exact location and campus
- Date lost/found/returned
- Contact information (clickable email and phone)
- Additional notes
- For returned items: owner and finder information with success story

## My Reports

- Your submitted reports are tracked automatically
- View them in the sidebar on Report pages
- Reports persist in your browser session
- Click on any report to view full details

## Success Stories

- Click "History of Returns" to see successful reunions
- Get inspired by community success stories
- See how the system helps people reconnect with their belongings

## Tips for Best Results

### When Reporting Lost Items
- Be as specific as possible in the description
- Include unique identifying features
- Mention the last known location
- Provide accurate contact information
- Check found items regularly

### When Reporting Found Items
- Describe the item accurately
- Note exactly where you found it
- Include any identifying marks or features
- Provide your contact information
- Check lost items to see if someone is looking for it

### When Searching
- Try different keywords
- Use the date range filter to narrow results
- Check both lost and found sections
- Look at similar categories
- Check regularly for new posts

## Contact Information

When viewing item details:
- Email addresses are clickable (opens your email client)
- Phone numbers are clickable (opens your phone app on mobile)
- Always be respectful and verify ownership before returning items

## Privacy & Safety

- Only share contact information you're comfortable with
- Meet in public, well-lit areas for item returns
- Verify ownership by asking for specific details about the item
- Report any suspicious activity to campus security
